Differing from its peers, the Sophos AP6 420X is designed for outdoor use, ensuring reliable Wi-Fi 6 connectivity in more challenging environments. Though it operates on the standard 2.4 and 5 GHz bands, its outdoor capability sets it apart. The model also benefits from the 2.5G interface, promoting faster LAN connectivity. Its robust design combined with the advantage of centralized management through Sophos Central makes it a suitable choice for diverse outdoor network requirements.
