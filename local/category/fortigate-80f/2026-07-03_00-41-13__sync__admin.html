FortiGate 80F / 81F FAQ

The 80F and 81F share the same firewall chip, throughput specs, and security feature set. The 81F adds two PoE-capable GE RJ45 LAN ports and a built-in 128 GB SSD for local logging and FortiSandbox Cloud storage. If you need to power PoE devices like IP phones or wireless access points directly from the firewall, or want onboard log retention without a separate FortiAnalyzer, the 81F is the right pick.

Fortinet recommends the 80F and 81F for environments with 25 to 100 users. Both models fit comfortably in branch offices, retail locations, and small business headquarters where you need real UTM throughput without rackmount hardware or the cost of a mid-range chassis.
