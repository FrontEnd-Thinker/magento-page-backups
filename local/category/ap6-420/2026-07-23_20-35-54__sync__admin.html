The Sophos AP6 420 stands as an indoor access point leveraging the power of Wi-Fi 6 technology. It caters to wireless connectivity needs over the 2.4 and 5 GHz bands, making it suitable for standard network requirements. Unlike other models in the series, the AP6 420 lacks the 2.5G interface for enhanced LAN connectivity. Its integration with the broader Sophos ecosystem ensures that management is streamlined, with the capability to oversee the device through Sophos Central.
