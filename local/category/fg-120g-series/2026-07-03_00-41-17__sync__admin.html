Fortinet FortiGate 120G / 121G. Distributed-enterprise NGFW with SP5-accelerated threat protection.

The FortiGate 120G and 121G are 1U rack firewalls built for distributed enterprise sites, branch offices, and WAN edges that handle real traffic. Powered by Fortinet's SP5 security processing unit, they reach 39 Gbps of firewall throughput and 2.8 Gbps of full-stack threat protection without giving up port density: 18 GE RJ45 ports, 8 GE SFP slots, and four 10GE SFP+ uplinks ship standard. Both models run FortiOS with built-in SD-WAN and ZTNA enforcement, work with the full FortiGuard services catalog, and include dual redundant AC power supplies for always-on operation.

Deciding between the FortiGate 120G and 121G?

The hardware, port configuration, and performance are identical on both models. The only difference is onboard storage. The 121G adds a 480 GB SSD for local logging, reporting, and traffic capture. If you forward logs to FortiAnalyzer or FortiGate Cloud, the 120G covers you. If you want logs stored on the appliance itself, choose the 121G. FortiGate 120G / 121G FAQ

The two models share identical hardware, port configuration, and performance specs. The only difference is onboard storage. The 121G includes a 480 GB SSD for local logging, reporting, and traffic capture directly on the appliance. The 120G does not include onboard storage. If you centralize logs through FortiAnalyzer or FortiGate Cloud, the 120G handles that just fine. If you want logs retained on the device itself, choose the 121G.

The 120G series delivers 39 Gbps of firewall throughput, 5.3 Gbps of IPS throughput, 3.1 Gbps of NGFW throughput, and 2.8 Gbps of full-stack threat protection. SSL inspection runs at 3 Gbps. All figures are SP5 ASIC-accelerated and sourced from the official Fortinet datasheet measured under lab conditions.

Three FortiGuard bundles are sold with the hardware. ATP includes IPS, advanced malware protection, and application control. UTP adds URL filtering, DNS filtering, video filtering, and antispam. Enterprise Protection adds data loss prevention and OT security services on top of UTP. All three bundles include FortiCare Premium support. Subscriptions are available in 1, 3, and 5 year terms.
