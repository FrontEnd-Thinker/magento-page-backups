What Sets SonicWall Apart

Real-Time Deep Packet Inspection

SonicWall's patented RTDMI and RFDPI engines inspect every packet at line speed, including encrypted SSL/TLS traffic. Threats are identified and blocked before they reach the network, not after the session closes.

One Platform from SMB to Enterprise

The TZ Series protects a 10-person office the same way the NSsp protects a 10,000-user data center. Policies, reporting, and management all live in SonicWall NSM, so your team runs one console regardless of site count.

SonicWall hardware ships in desktop, 1U rack, and high-availability chassis form factors. The NSv runs on VMware, Hyper-V, KVM, AWS, and Azure. Every deployment model is covered, and every model runs the same OS.
