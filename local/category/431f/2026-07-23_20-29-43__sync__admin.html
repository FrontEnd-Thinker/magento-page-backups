<p>Cloud or FortiOS-managed Wi-Fi 6 wireless access points with single-pane-of-glass management. This enterprise class 802.11ax (Wi-Fi 6) indoor APs provide three radios. The top-of-the-line access points provide OFDMA, a 2.5 Gigabit Ethernet port, plus an additional 1 Gbps Ethernet port for PoE diversity. The APs can provide 24/7 scanning across both bands while still providing access on both the 2.4 and 5 GHz bands. The integrated BLE radio can be used for beacons and locationing applications.</p
