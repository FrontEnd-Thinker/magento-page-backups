71F vs. 70F

The 70F and 71F share the same performance specs, port layout, and Security Fabric feature set. The 71F adds a 128 GB SSD for on-device log storage, which removes the need for a separate FortiAnalyzer in compliance-driven environments. If your logging goes to the cloud or a dedicated collector, the 70F covers every other use case at a lower price point.

Not sure the 70F is the right fit?

The 70F and 71F sit between the FortiGate 60F below and the FortiGate 80F above. If your inspected throughput requirement exceeds 1 Gbps, or your site runs more than 100 concurrent users, the 80F or 100F is the better call. Our engineers can run the numbers based on your WAN speed and user count.
