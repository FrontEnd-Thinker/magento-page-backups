Purpose-Built for MSPs & SMBs

WatchGuard was designed from the ground up for managed service providers and small-to-midsize businesses. Role-based access, multi-tenant management, and transparent pricing make it easy to deploy, bill, and scale security services profitably.

Total Security Suite — No Trade-Offs

Proven Zero-Trust Endpoint Defense

WatchGuard EPDR applies a Zero-Trust Application Service that classifies every running process — trusted, malware, or unknown — before execution, blocking threats that evade traditional signature-based antivirus tools and providing continuous post-execution monitoring.
