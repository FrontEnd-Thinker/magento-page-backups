TZ280 FAQ

The TZ280 is the base model with 8 GbE copper ports and 2 SFP fiber uplinks. The TZ280W adds an integrated Wi-Fi 6 (2x2, 802.11ax) radio so you can serve wireless clients directly from the firewall without a separate access point. The TZ280P adds PoE+ ports that can power SonicWave access points, IP phones, and other powered devices over the same cable that carries data. All three run SonicOS 8 and support the same security service subscriptions, so the choice comes down to your physical environment.
