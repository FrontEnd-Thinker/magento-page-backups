Why Fortinet

What Sets Fortinet Apart

The Security Fabric

Every Fortinet product shares a common architecture called the Security Fabric. Firewalls, switches, access points, endpoint clients, and management tools share threat intelligence and policy information natively, so your network responds to threats without manual coordination between disconnected tools.

One Operating System Across the Portfolio

FortiOS runs on every FortiGate appliance, from a desktop branch unit to a high-availability campus core firewall. Your team learns one interface, one CLI, and one policy model. Those skills carry across every device you add to the environment, which cuts training time and reduces configuration errors.

Purpose-Built Security Processors

Fortinet designs its own NP and CP ASICs to handle encryption, deep packet inspection, and traffic processing in dedicated hardware instead of software running on general-purpose CPUs. The result is higher firewall throughput at a lower per-unit cost than competing platforms doing the same inspection work in software.

FortiGuard AI Threat Intelligence

FortiGuard Labs is one of the largest threat research teams in the industry. Updated signatures, behavioral detection rules, and zero-day intelligence flow to every subscribed Fortinet device automatically. Your defenses stay current without manual signature management or waiting on a vendor patch cycle.
