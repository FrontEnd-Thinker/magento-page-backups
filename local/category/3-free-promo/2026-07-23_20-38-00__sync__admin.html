3 & Free at a Glance

What Is Included

Each 3 & Free bundle includes a new SonicWall Gen 7 firewall appliance, 3 years of Essential Protection Security Suite (gateway antivirus, intrusion prevention, content filtering, Capture ATP, cloud management, and 24x7 support), plus 1 free year of SonicWall Secure Internet Access Basic for 10 users and 1 free year of SonicWall Secure Private Access Advanced for 3 users. The free remote access licenses activate with the same subscription start date as the firewall services.

Trade-In Requirement

The 3 & Free promotion is a trade-up offer, meaning you need to have an existing SonicWall (typically Gen 5 or Gen 6) or a qualifying competitive firewall in order to take advantage of the pricing. The trade-in does not have to be the same model you are upgrading to. If you are unsure whether your current device qualifies, contact our team and we will check for you before you place your order.

Cloud Secure Edge Explained

The free SIA Basic and SPA Advanced licenses give your users secure, zero-trust access to the internet and internal applications from anywhere, without a traditional VPN. SIA Basic provides DNS-layer web filtering and threat protection for remote users. SPA Advanced delivers application-level access control so users can reach only the resources they are authorized for. Both work alongside your Gen 7 firewall to extend protection beyond the perimeter.
