Fortinet Wireless Firewall FAQ

A Fortinet FortiGate Wireless Firewall, sold under the FortiWiFi product name, is a compact desktop appliance that combines a full FortiGate next-generation firewall with a built-in Wi-Fi access point. One device handles routing, deep packet inspection, VPN, SD-WAN, and wireless connectivity for the office. Everything runs through a single FortiOS interface, so there is no need for a separate wireless controller or a second management console.

The three models scale in throughput and user capacity. The FWF-30G is sized for small offices up to 25 users. The FWF-50G handles growing offices of 25 to 50 users with more headroom for concurrent sessions and inspection workloads. The FWF-70G and 71G step up to medium branch environments of 50 to 100 users and add dual-radio Wi-Fi 6E for faster wireless throughput. The 71G additionally includes PoE ports for powering IP phones or cameras without a separate switch.
