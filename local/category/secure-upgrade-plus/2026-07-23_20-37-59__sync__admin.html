What You Get

Every Secure Upgrade Plus bundle includes a new SonicWall Gen 7 firewall appliance plus a multi-year Essential, Threat, or Advanced Protection Security Suite subscription. Services cover gateway antivirus, intrusion prevention, content filtering, application intelligence, Capture ATP sandbox, cloud management, and 24x7 technical support, all bundled in at a single discounted price.

Who Is Eligible

Any organization with an existing SonicWall Gen 5 or Gen 6 firewall or a qualifying competitive appliance can participate. The old device is traded in as part of the purchase, and SonicWall issues the upgrade pricing accordingly. Contact our team if you are unsure whether your current firewall qualifies before placing your order.

Choosing an Edition

Essential Edition covers the core threat stack: content filtering, gateway antivirus, intrusion prevention, Capture ATP, and cloud management. Threat Edition adds application control and DNS filtering. Advanced Edition layers on Network Security Manager, advanced reporting, and extended analytics. Most small and mid-size businesses start with Essential or Threat and upgrade as their security requirements grow.
