FortiGate 90G / 91G at a Glance

Performance

Firewall throughput up to 27.9 Gbps. IPS throughput 4.5 Gbps. NGFW throughput 2.5 Gbps. Threat protection 2.2 Gbps. SSL inspection throughput 2.6 Gbps. Application control throughput 6.7 Gbps. New sessions per second 124,000. Concurrent sessions 3 million. Powered by the Fortinet FortiSP5 ASIC with hardware-accelerated IPsec VPN at 25 Gbps.

Interfaces and Connectivity

8x GE RJ45 internal ports. 2x shared-media WAN ports switchable between 10 GE RJ45 or 10GE/GE SFP+ per slot. 1x USB management port. 1x RJ45 console. Supports up to 24 FortiSwitches and 96 FortiAPs (48 in tunnel mode). The 91G adds a single 120 GB internal SSD; the 90G ships without onboard storage.

VPN and Zero-Trust Access

IPsec VPN throughput 25 Gbps. Up to 200 gateway-to-gateway tunnels and 2,500 client-to-gateway tunnels. SSL VPN throughput 1.4 Gbps with up to 200 concurrent tunnel-mode users. Universal ZTNA enforcement is built into FortiOS and controls application access for both on-site and remote users without a separate gateway.

Management and High Availability

Active-Active, Active-Passive, and full clustering HA. Dedicated front-panel HA port for direct unit pairing. Hardware Trusted Platform Module (TPM) for cryptographic key storage. Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) for zero-touch provisioning. Centralized management via FortiManager or FortiGate Cloud. Virtual domains: 10 default, 10 maximum.

FortiGate 90G / 91G FAQ

The only hardware difference is internal storage. The 91G ships with a 120 GB SSD for local logging, WAN optimization, and FortiGuard database caching. The 90G has the same ports, FortiSP5 ASIC, and performance numbers but no onboard storage. Both models accept the same FortiGuard service bundles and FortiCare support tiers, so the licensing and subscription paths are identical.

Fortinet rates the 90G series for environments with 200 to 500 users. Actual headroom depends heavily on which security services you run. Full threat protection with SSL inspection draws more resources than a basic firewall policy. Our engineers can help you size the right model against your actual traffic mix, concurrent session count, and service requirements before you order.

Fortinet offers three main service tiers for the 90G and 91G. Advanced Threat Protection covers IPS, malware protection, and application control. Unified Threat Protection adds URL filtering, DNS security, and antispam on top of that. The Enterprise Protection bundle is the most complete, adding inline CASB, DLP, AI-powered inline sandbox, attack surface monitoring, and the FortiConverter migration service. All tiers are available in one-, three-, and five-year terms bundled with FortiCare Premium support.

For distributed sites where a redundant pair is not practical, the 90G also works with FortiManager zero-touch provisioning, so a replacement unit can pull its full configuration automatically without requiring on-site IT staff to intervene.
