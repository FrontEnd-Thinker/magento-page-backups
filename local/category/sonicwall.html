Why SonicWall

What Sets SonicWall Apart

Deep Packet Inspection

Every SonicWall firewall inspects every byte of every packet without proxying or reassembly. That includes encrypted TLS 1.3 traffic on Gen 8 models. No other vendor at this price point offers the same depth of inspection across the entire product line.

Capture ATP Sandboxing

SonicWall's cloud sandbox uses Real Time Deep Memory Inspection (RTDMI) to catch zero day malware before it enters your network. Files are analyzed in memory before they execute, delivering verdicts in under two seconds without impacting throughput.

Network Security Manager

Manage one firewall or a thousand from a single cloud dashboard. NSM 3.0 gives you centralized policy, firmware, and reporting across all sites. Deploy config changes to every device at once or stage rollouts by location.

Platinum Partner Support

We are a SonicWall Platinum partner and DMR Partner of the Year. Our certified engineers deploy, configure, and manage SonicWall firewalls for businesses daily. When you buy from us, you get an engineering team that knows these devices inside and out.
