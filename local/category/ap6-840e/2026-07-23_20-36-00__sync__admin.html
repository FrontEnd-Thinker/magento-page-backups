The Sophos AP6 840E is a top-tier indoor access point that encapsulates the benefits of both Wi-Fi 6 and Wi-Fi 6E. It's designed to offer wireless connectivity over the 2.4, 5, and the coveted 6 GHz bands, providing users with wider bandwidth options and better performance. The inclusion of a 2.5G interface ensures faster LAN speeds, avoiding potential network bottlenecks. It retains the seamless integration with Sophos Central for comprehensive management capabilities.
