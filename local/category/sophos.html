Why Sophos

What Sets Sophos Apart

Xstream Architecture

XGS firewalls split traffic between a hardware-accelerated FastPath and a single deep-packet inspection engine that handles TLS 1.3, IPS, app control, and antivirus together. There is no stacking overhead, so performance stays consistent with all protection modules enabled. Each SFOS update improves throughput without requiring a hardware upgrade.

Synchronized Security

When Sophos Intercept X detects a compromised endpoint, the XGS firewall automatically isolates that device before the threat can spread, with no manual intervention needed. Firewalls, switches, and access points share health status in real time through Sophos Central, so your network responds to active threats as a connected system rather than a collection of separate appliances.

One Console for Everything

Sophos Central brings your firewalls, access points, switches, and endpoint protection into one cloud dashboard. There is no separate management platform for each product line. Firmware updates, policy changes, and reporting all happen in one place, keeping your full security posture visible at a glance.

Value at Every Tier

Sophos prices the XGS Series to deliver a competitive cost per protected megabit at every tier. Trusted by more than 600,000 customers worldwide and rated 4.7 out of 5 in the Gartner Peer Insights 2024 Network Firewalls report. Each SFOS release adds new protections to your existing hardware without a forklift upgrade.

The Sophos SD-RED, or Remote Ethernet Device, is a compact hardware appliance that extends your main office network to a branch location over an AES-256 encrypted tunnel. You enter the device ID into your XGS firewall, ship the SD-RED to the remote site, and plug it in. It automatically contacts your firewall and establishes a secure connection with no technical staff needed at the branch. The SD-RED 20 covers small offices and retail locations with four LAN ports. The SD-RED 60 adds PoE ports, higher throughput, and optional 3G/4G connectivity for larger branch offices.
