FortiGate 60F / 61F FAQ

The hardware, processors, and throughput specs are identical on both models. The only difference is that the 61F includes a 128 GB internal SSD. That storage enables on-box logging, local quarantine of threat files, and extended FortiGuard intelligence retention without a separate FortiAnalyzer or cloud logging subscription. If you need local logs for compliance or do not have a log management appliance in your stack, the 61F is worth the small price difference.

Fortinet rates the 60F and 61F for environments with approximately 25 to 75 concurrent users. The practical limit depends more on your traffic mix than raw headcount. A site with heavy video conferencing or large file transfers will approach the threat protection ceiling (700 Mbps) well before the firewall throughput ceiling (10 Gbps). If your internet circuit runs above 500 Mbps and you enable full UTM inspection, talk to one of our engineers before purchasing so we can confirm the fit.
