An advancement in the series, the Sophos AP6 420E is an indoor access point that operates not only on Wi-Fi 6 but also extends its reach to Wi-Fi 6E. This model offers wireless connections across the 2.4, 5, and the extended 6 GHz bands where available. The 2.5G interface ensures users benefit from faster LAN connectivity, and like its counterparts, it's seamlessly integrated into the Sophos Central for easy management and monitoring.
